## Description

In lightweight-charts v5, `ISeriesApi` does NOT have a `setMarkers()` method directly. Markers are a separate **plugin** API: `createSeriesMarkers(series, markers?)` returns an `ISeriesMarkersPluginApi` which has `setMarkers()`, `markers()`, and `detach()`.

Also exists: `createUpDownMarkers(series, options?)` for `ISeriesUpDownMarkerPluginApi` (line/area series only).

## Evidence

- Session 68e8a9c8: `this.candleSeries.setMarkers([])` → TS2339 error. Fixed by importing `createSeriesMarkers` + `ISeriesMarkersPluginApi`, creating plugin with `createSeriesMarkers(this.candleSeries, [])`, storing as `markersPlugin`, and calling `this.markersPlugin.setMarkers(markers)`.

## Decision

**Discarded** 2026-06-27.
Already covered in `/memories/library-api-versions.md` (lightweight-charts v5
section documents `createSeriesMarkers()` API). No new knowledge to promote.
